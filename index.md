### Bio

I am a Research Scientist at [Fauna Robotics](https://faunarobotics.com/) (acquired by Amazon in 2026), where I lead perception and loco-manipulation for autonomous robots. We recently released our first humanoid robot named Sprout and published a [technical report](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.18963). 

Previously, I was at [Zipline](https://www.zipline.com/), where I led the development of vision-based detection for airborne and ground obstacles for autonomous long-range drone delivery. I drove the technical strategy and solutions for a Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) submission, with a primary focus on enabling BVLOS (Beyond Visual Line of Sight) operations.

I started my machine learning career at WhatsApp on the anti-abuse team by training NLP models to detect harmful groups within WhatsApp’s end-to-end encrypted environment.

I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Michigan, with a focus on Computer Architecture and Compilers.
